Masonic Art

Daylight lodge is a Masonic lodge located in Seattle. Our lodge has a unique heritage related to the arts and of providing Masonry to those who either work evenings or prefer to attend lodge on weekend mornings. Our members come from a wide range of backgrounds and interests. Good fellowship, good victuals and good drinks are a fundamental nature of the lodge.

Founded in 1920 predominantly by entertainers, Daylight follows the objectives of the Masonic philosophy which includes the study of the liberal arts and sciences and the practice of brotherly love, relief and truth.

Our lodge sponsors many community activities including:

  • Fremont Solstice Festival Bandstand
  • Seattle Youth Symphony Annual Dinner
  • Many More Events!
